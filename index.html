About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Maryan, a highly creative and detail-oriented UX/UI designer with a genuine passion for crafting exceptional digital experiences. I love pushing my skills by actively participating in hackathons, where I get to explore innovative solutions and stay on the cutting edge of design. For me, simplicity is key. I firmly believe that keeping things simple leads to a smoother user experience, making it easier for people to connect with digital products effortlessly. I always put the user first, caring deeply about their needs and preferences, ensuring that my designs resonate and deliver value. In my work, I follow a rigorous 5-step design process. From empathizing with users and defining their pain points to ideating, prototyping, and delivering polished interfaces, I'm committed to creating intuitive and user-friendly designs that leave a lasting impact. Let's collaborate and create something amazing together! Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out to discuss how we can make your project stand out with a delightful user experience.
